FAQ

email alex@orkiv.com if you've got more questions and we'll do our best to answer.

  • Do I need to buy onboarding services?

    No. Not all plans require an onboarding service. For our starter, professional and enterprise plans there is a one-time $199 onboarding fee to help setup StoreHub, integrate data, and make sure you get off on the right foot!

    We want to help all clients succeed, so after lot’s of struggle we’ve noticed it drastically increases your chances of succeeding with StoreHub. 

  • Do I need to pay yearly for StoreHub?

    Our Starter, Professional and Enterprise plans are all paid in yearly amounts. We find that committing to a year will set you up for success since marketing and growing your retail or eCommerce business is a long-term commitment. Beyond this, our customers are happy when they do well, and we want happy customers and we’ve found pricing yearly helps enable this.

  • What support will I receive?

    All Professional and Enterprise customers will have a default account manager assigned to them, and will receive support within 24 hours of a submitted inquiry.

    For the Essentials and Starter plans, we review and help with support requests in the order they were received. You can always view our docs, or ask the community for help.

    Additionally for immediate support you can choose the $199 support plan which dedicates up to 5 hours per month for dedicated support, marketing, and technical help. 

  • What is the data cap? How much is it to buy more?

    Each account includes 1 gigabyte of data which is equal to one of these:
    3,000 web pages
    4,000 photos
    10,000 emails
    310 minutes of YouTube

    Each GB of data is $5/month thereafter

  • Are there any overage fees?

    No. You are only charged based on the plan you have selected and the amount of data you are using within the platform. The amount of data being used will scale up with you as you scale and have more customers using the platform. It is part of our commitment to staying competitively priced with great value to each customer.

  • Are there any limits?

    Our infrastructure is built on state of the art Google Cloud platform which will scale infinitely. The sky's the limit here, just note, each 1GB you use will continue to increase the billable amount for that month.
